Bihar’s Historical Forts: Echoes of the Past

Exploring the legacy of mighty fortresses across Bihar.

Bihar’s history lives not just in books but in the towering forts that still echo with tales of its past. These historical monuments stand as a testimony to the region’s bravery, strategy, and royal legacy.

The Rohtasgarh Fort, located on the Kaimur plateau, was a strategic military base used by Sher Shah Suri. It is known for its massive gates and natural defense structures. Darbhanga Fort is a symbol of Mithila architecture and royal heritage, located in the heart of the city of Darbhanga.

Munger Fort, built during the Slave Dynasty, played a key role during various battles and administrative regimes. The partially ruined Rajnagar Fort still showcases the artistic and cultural brilliance of the region.

Visiting these forts is like traveling back in time. They offer insight into Bihar’s political, military, and cultural journey. These forts are not only historically significant but are also architectural masterpieces that Bihar takes pride in.
