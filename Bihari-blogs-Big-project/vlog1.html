Bodh Gaya: The Land of Enlightenment

One of the holiest sites in Bihar and the world.

Bodh Gaya is where Prince Siddhartha attained enlightenment under the Bodhi Tree and became the Buddha. It's located in Gaya district of Bihar. The Mahabodhi Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage site, attracts millions of visitors every year from across the globe.

Apart from its religious significance, the town is rich in culture, local food, and peaceful environment that makes it a perfect spiritual getaway. Several monasteries built by countries like Japan, Thailand, Bhutan, and Sri Lanka can also be found here.

Whether you're spiritual or just exploring, Bodh Gaya offers a unique mix of divinity and culture you won't forget.
